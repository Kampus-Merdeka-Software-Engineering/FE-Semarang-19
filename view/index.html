Services

Regular

Regular delivery service is a standard option that offers delivery of goods in a time that usually takes a few days or more, depending on the distance and destination of the shipment. This service is suitable for shipments that do not require immediate delivery and are prioritized to save costs.

Express

Express delivery services are options designed to deliver items quickly. Express delivery can reach the destination within one to two business days, depending on the location and service provider. This service is suitable for situations where speed of delivery is critical, such as urgent document deliveries or items that need to arrive quickly.

Same Day

Same day delivery service is the fastest option, where the item or package is delivered on the same day as the shipment. This is the fastest solution for very urgent delivery needs. This service can only be used for deliveries within the same province.
